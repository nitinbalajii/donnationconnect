Become a Volunteer

Join our network of volunteers and help bridge the gap between donors and those in need.

Personal Information

Tell us about yourself so we can match you with the right volunteer opportunities.

Volunteer Preferences

Let us know how you'd like to help and when you're available.

Pick up and deliver donations using your own vehicle

Collect and deliver food donations

Organize donation drives and community events

Help at partner shelter homes with sorting and distribution

Additional Information

Help us understand your skills and background better.

Why Volunteer With Us?

  • Make a direct impact in your local community
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your availability
  • Meet like-minded people who want to help others
  • Gain valuable experience and skills
  • Receive volunteer certification and recognition

Volunteer Process

  1. Apply: Complete and submit the volunteer application
  2. Interview: Brief phone or video interview with our volunteer coordinator
  3. Orientation: Attend a volunteer orientation session
  4. Background Check: Complete a background check (for certain roles)
  5. Start Volunteering: Begin making a difference in your community

Questions?

If you have any questions about volunteering with us, please contact our volunteer coordinator.

volunteer@donationconnect.org (123) 456-7890
